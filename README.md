## üìö About This Project

This repository documents my journey learning electronics, from the most basic concepts (what is charge?) to more advanced topics. Everything is explained in plain English with practical examples, analogies, and hands-on exercises.

**Target Audience:** Complete beginners with no prior electronics or physics knowledge

**Approach:** Learn by understanding, not memorizing. Every concept is explained with:
- Clear, jargon-free explanations
- Real-world analogies (water flow, gravity, everyday examples)
- Practical calculations and examples
- Visual diagrams and tables
- Practice problems with solutions

## üéØ Learning Philosophy

- **Start from atoms** - Understand what electricity actually is
- **Build incrementally** - Each concept builds on previous ones
- **Practice actively** - Do calculations, build circuits, measure things
- **Use analogies** - Make abstract concepts concrete
- **Be beginner-friendly** - No assumed knowledge

- **Charge and Atoms** - What electricity is at the atomic level
- **Electric Fields** - Invisible force zones around charges
- **Ions and Charge Separation** - How charges get separated
- **Battery Working** - How batteries create and maintain voltage
- **Voltage** - Electrical pressure that pushes electrons
- **Current** - The flow of electrons through conductors
- **Resistance** - Opposition to current flow
- **Ohm's Law** - The most important formula: V = I √ó R
- **Power** - Energy used per second, heating, and efficiency
- **Units and Conversions** - Working with mA, kŒ©, mW, and more
